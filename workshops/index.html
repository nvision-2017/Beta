ηvision, IIT Hyderabad announces the first workshop of this year. As the name suggests, this time its an industrial visit to a CocaCola plant.

ηvision, IIT Hyderabad in association with Robokart technologies presents you with an oppurtunity to visit the plant and witness cool automated process like manufacturing, bottling & dispatch.
